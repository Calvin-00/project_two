Educational and Career Consultation

A better future for the children is the concern and best wish of each parent. The selection of the academic pathway and future career is sometimes not an easy task. At AquaGHG, we help parents and children make the right choices that meet the market demand, personal ability, and keen interest.

We personally guide parents and children in choosing undergraduate and postgraduate studies in different universities based on the financial status of the applicants. In addition, we help applicants to identify and apply to universities renowned for their Quality Education and Research. Applicants are advised based on whether they are seeking full scholarships, partial scholarships, or are self-sponsored students. Applicants are accompanied from seeking admission until they obtain their visa and settle in their respective universities of choice. They are also given mentorship throughout their academic cursus.

AquaGHG Consult also provides consultancy services in course or program development.
