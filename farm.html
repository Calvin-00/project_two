About AquaGHG Farm

AquaGHG Farm is located in the Rwamagana District, Eastern Province of Rwanda. We specialize in the production of Bio Milk, Bio Meat from goats, sheep, rabbits, and chicken, as well as Bio Eggs. Our commitment to ecological farming ensures that all our animals are fed natural fodder.

We pride ourselves on producing and selling only Bio Meat and Bio Eggs, with our chickens being primarily local breeds. Our Bio Milk production is currently at a low scale and used for our own consumption.

At AquaGHG Farm, we practice Ecological Animal Husbandry by feeding our animals with natural fodder and conducting research to reintroduce Rwandan traditional knowledge in veterinary medicine.

Zero Waste Principle

Our principal is zero waste. Bio wastes from our farm are used to produce Biogas for cooking and electricity production. The biogas sludge is then used to fertilize our horticulture crops, ensuring a sustainable and eco-friendly farming practice.

Community and Training

We have established a center to revitalize small household farm biogas systems in Rwanda. Our training programs help small farmers efficiently manage their farms to improve livelihoods and contribute to the school feeding scheme. We work closely with nine and twelve basic education schools to maintain and rehabilitate small household farms, biogas systems, and common cow sheds management.
